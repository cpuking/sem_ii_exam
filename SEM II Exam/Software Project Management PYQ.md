## 🔹 d) Quality Planning vs Quality Assurance

**Answer:**

|---|---|

|Done before execution|Done during execution|

## 🔹 Q2(a) Explain Capability Maturity Model (CMM)

**Answer (4 marks):**

CMM is a framework used to **improve software processes**.

### 📊 Levels of CMM:

1. **Level 1 – Initial**
    
    - No proper process
        
    - Chaotic development
        
2. **Level 2 – Repeatable**
    
    - Basic project management exists
        
3. **Level 3 – Defined**
    
    - Standard processes across organization
        
4. **Level 4 – Managed**
    
    - Processes are measured and controlled
        
5. **Level 5 – Optimizing**
    
    - Continuous improvement

## 🔹 Quality Assurance vs Quality Control

**Answer (4 marks):**

- **QA:** Process-oriented (prevention)
    
- **QC:** Product-oriented (detection)
    

Example:

- QA → Process guidelines
    
- QC → Testing software
